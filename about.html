About Us

The beginning of making classical guitars was preceded by my great love for this instrument, as well as playing classical music as a hobby. Same as with a large number of builders, the starting point for the construction is an attempt to repair one instrument. In my case, that was a guitar by the famous German builder (Dieter Hopf) and an attempt to return the guitar into a functional state. From that moment, I have started to search for top-quality materials, construction techniques, builds, and tools. Dov developed a great passion for violin-making as a result of many visits to Italy. and through his friendship with Maestro Liutaio Francesco Bissolotti in Cremona (shown seated below with Dov.). The Maestro also helped Dov appreciate the great qualities of instruments created during the recent revival of the classical method of violin making in that historic city.

Dov founded Dov Schmidt Music to offer string players and violin-makers around the world top-quality products at the lowest possible prices.
